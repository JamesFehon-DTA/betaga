<p>The <em>Benefits management policy </em>(BMP) defines how benefits must be managed for digital and ICT-enabled investments. The Policy:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>ensures that agencies understand the requirements to successfully deliver the outcomes that Australians need </li><li>enables effective oversight and reporting of investment outcomes across the Government's&nbsp;digital and ICT investment portfolio.</li></ul><p>The Australian Government has endorsed the application of the BMP for digital and ICT proposals coming forward for Cabinet consideration under the <a href="https://www.dta.gov.au/help-and-advice/digital-and-ict-investments/digital-and-ict-investment-oversight-framework">Digital and ICT Investment Oversight Framework</a>.</p><h2>Access the policy</h2><p>View the <a href="https://architecture.digital.gov.au/sites/default/files/2025-10/Benefits_management_policy.pdf"><em>Benefits management policy </em>(full text)</a>.</p><h2>Applicability</h2><p>Unless formally excepted, the BMP applies where digital and ICT investment:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>is brought forward by a non-corporate Commonwealth entity and, where specifically requested by the Minister responsible for the Digital Transformation Agency, a Corporate Commonwealth entity</li><li>involves digital or ICT costs</li><li>is being brought forward for government consideration as a new policy proposal.</li></ul>

## Policy requirements

### Requirement 1

Benefits are aligned to strategic objectives.

### Requirement 2

Agencies that deliver projects adopt a benefits-led culture and approach to change.

### Requirement 3

Benefit dependencies are explicitly understood and recorded.

### Requirement 4

Benefits are measurable, and evidence based.

### Requirement 5

Benefits are integrated into a project's governance approach.

### Requirement 6

Benefits are integrated into performance management.

### Requirement 7

Benefits are owned by business units, and not by the project.

### Requirement 8

Benefits management activities are integrated into project management activities.
