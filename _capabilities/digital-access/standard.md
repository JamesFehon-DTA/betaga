<p>Direct link: <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-access-standard"><em>digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-access-standard</em></a><br>Responsible agency: <strong>Digital Transformation Agency</strong><br>Last updated: <strong>18 November 2025</strong></p><h2>About the standard</h2><p>The <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-access-standard">Digital access standard</a> sets criteria for agencies to reduce duplication of entry points, supporting unified access to digital government services for people and business.</p><p>It helps agencies consider consolidating access points by either incorporating a new service into an existing platform or creating a new access point and migrating other existing access points into it.</p><h2>Applicability</h2><p>Investments that are subject to the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/investment">Investment oversight framework (IOF)</a> must meet this standard. Check the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience">Digital experience policy</a> for details and exemptions.</p><h2>Access the standard</h2><p>Read the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-access-standard">Digital access standard</a>.</p>
