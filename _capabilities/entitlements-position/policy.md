<p>An entitlements policy will be developed as part of the ongoing maturation of the Australian Government Architecture. To provide input, please contact <a href="mailto:architecture@dta.gov.au"><u>architecture@dta.gov.au</u></a>.&nbsp;</p><h2>Applicability &nbsp;</h2><p>Digital investment proposals are assessed against this policy by the DTA through the <a href="https://www.dta.gov.au/advice/digital-and-ict-investments/digital-and-ict-investment-oversight-framework-iof"><u>Digital and ICT Investment Oversight Framework (IOF)</u></a>.&nbsp;</p><p>This entitlements policy (Position) may be useful to Commonwealth entities investing in entitlement solutions.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>This Federated Authentication<strong> </strong>policy position represents the DTA’s opinion of best practice and will be further refined upon feedback provided.</p>

## Policy requirements

### Requirement 1

Avoid investments in new or standalone entitlements solutions

### Requirement 2

Reuse existing investment in entitlements systems

### Requirement 3

Where possible, centralise entitlements management processes into a single digital platform.

### Requirement 4

Comply with relevant legislation and regulations.
