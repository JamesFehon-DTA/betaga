<p>Direct link: <a href="https://digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-service-standard"><em><u>digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-service-standard</u></em></a><br>Responsible agency: <strong>Digital Transformation Agency</strong><br>Last updated: <strong>18 November 2025</strong></p><h2>About the standard</h2><p>The <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-service-standard">Digital service standard</a> sets the requirements for designing and delivering digital government services.</p><p>It guides digital teams to create and maintain digital services that are:</p><ul><li>user-friendly</li><li>inclusive</li><li>adaptable</li><li>measurable.</li></ul><h2>Applicability</h2><p>Investments that are subject to the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/investment">Investment oversight framework (IOF)</a> and all existing services must meet this standard. Check the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience">Digital experience policy</a> for details and exemptions.</p><h2>Access the standard</h2><p>Read the <a href="https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-service-standard">Digital service standard</a>.</p>
