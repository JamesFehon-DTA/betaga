Direct link: digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience Responsible agency: Digital Transformation Agency Last updated: 18 November 2025 About the policy The Digital experience policy supports a whole-of-government focus on improving the experience for people and business interacting digitally with government information and services. It sets agreed benchmarks for the performance of digital services and supports agencies to design and deliver better experiences by considering the broader digital service ecosystem. Applicability The policy is mandatory and applies to investments that are subject to the Investment oversight framework (IOF) . Check the policy for details and exemptions.
