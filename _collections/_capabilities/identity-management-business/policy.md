## Policy requirements

### Requirement 1

Use accredited providers through the Services Australia Identity Exchange under the Trusted Digital Identity Framework (TDIF) when verifying identity of Australian businesses or their authorised representatives

### Requirement 2

Avoid investment in new identity capabilities that are available as existing government assets

### Requirement 3

Ensure best practices are applied in building digital services
