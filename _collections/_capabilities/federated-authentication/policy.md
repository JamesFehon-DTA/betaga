A federated authentication policy will be developed as part of the ongoing maturation of the Australian Government Architecture. To provide input, please contact architecture@dta.gov.au . Applicability Digital investment proposals are assessed against this policy by the DTA through the Digital and ICT Investment Oversight Framework (IOF) . This federated authentication policy (position) may be useful to Commonwealth entities investing in entitlement solutions. This Federated Authentication policy position represents the DTAâ€™s opinion of best practice and will be further refined upon feedback provided.

## Policy requirements

### Requirement 1

Onboard to the Federated Authentication Service (FAS) as identity providers.

### Requirement 2

Onboard services to the FAS as relaying parties.

### Requirement 3

Avoid investment in new or standalone federated authentication solutions.
