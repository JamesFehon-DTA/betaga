Direct link: www.digital.gov.au/ai/ai-in-government-policy Responsible agency: Digital Transformation Agency Last updated: 1 December 2025 About the policy The Policy for the responsible use of AI in government supports the accelerated and sustainable adoption of AI across government. It aims to ensure agencies use of AI is responsible by: complementing and aligning with existing frameworks setting out requirements for AI strategy, oversight, preparedness, operations, and impact assessments. Applicability The policy is mandatory for non-corporate government departments and agencies. Check the policy for details and exemptions.
