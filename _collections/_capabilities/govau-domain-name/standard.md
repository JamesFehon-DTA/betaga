Direct link: [*www.domainname.gov.au/policies/policy-and-governance-framework*](http://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/policy-and-governance-framework)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Finance**  
  
This standard applies to all uses of the gov.au domain for web resources, including third level domains at the Australian Government level (e.g. example.gov.au) and fourth level domains at the state/territory/local government levels (e.g. example.act.gov.au).

## Access the standard

The domainname.gov.au website hosts the standard [*Policy and governance framework*.](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/policy-and-governance-framework)

## Apply the standard

Entities must:

Comply with each of the policies detailed in the [framework](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/policy-and-governance-framework). This includes:

* [*Eligibility and allocation policy*](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/eligibility-and-allocation-policy)
* [*Australian Government domain name policy*](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/australian-government-domain-name-policy)
* [*Dispute resolution policy*](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/dispute-resolution-policy)
* [*Application of auDA* policy](https://www.domainname.gov.au/policies/application-auda-policy)
