## About the standard

The [Standard for AI transparency statements](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/ai-in-government-policy/standard-ai-transparency-statements) sets out:

* what information agencies must include in the statement
* how agencies should publish the statement
* when agencies need to review and update the statement.

## Applicability

Non-corporate Commonwealth entities must meet this standard. Check the [Policy for the responsible use of AI in government](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/ai-in-government-policy) for details and exemptions.
