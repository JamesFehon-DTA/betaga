## About the standard

The [Standard for accountability](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/ai-in-government-policy/accountability) sets out:

* the requirement for government agencies to designate an official, or officials, to be responsible for the implementation of the Policy for responsible use of AI in government in their agency
* the requirement that each AI use case in scope of the policy has designated accountability
* the key responsibilities of accountable officials and accountable use case owners
* how to establish and share the internal use case register.

## Applicability

Non-corporate Commonwealth entities must meet this standard. Check the [Policy for the responsible use of AI in government](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/ai-in-government-policy) for details and exemptions.
