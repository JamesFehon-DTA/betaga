An entitlements policy will be developed as part of the ongoing maturation of the Australian Government Architecture. To provide input, please contact architecture@dta.gov.au . Applicability Digital investment proposals are assessed against this policy by the DTA through the Digital and ICT Investment Oversight Framework (IOF) . This entitlements policy (Position) may be useful to Commonwealth entities investing in entitlement solutions. ## Policy requirements

### Requirement 1

Avoid investments in new or standalone entitlements solutions

### Requirement 2

Reuse existing investment in entitlements systems

### Requirement 3

Where possible, centralise entitlements management processes into a single digital platform.

### Requirement 4

Comply with relevant legislation and regulations.
