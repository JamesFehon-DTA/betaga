## About the standard

The [Digital performance standard](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience/digital-performance-standard) sets out the approach to monitoring digital performance across government services and supporting data driven digital and ICT investment (ICT investment) decisions.

## Applicability

Investments that are subject to the [Investment oversight framework (IOF)](https://www.digital.gov.au/investment) must meet this standard. Check the [Digital experience policy](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/digital-experience) for details and exemptions.
