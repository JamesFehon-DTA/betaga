## Details

Direct link: [*www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/my-aged-care-service-and-support-portal*](http://www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/my-aged-care-service-and-support-portal)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Health and Aged Care**  
  
The [My aged care service and support portal](https://www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/my-aged-care-service-and-support-portal) enables approved aged care service providers to:

* enter and manage information about their services
* see their service’s care minutes target
* manage referrals
* update client records
* generate reports
* ask assessors to review a client’s support plan.

To access the portal, users need to:

* have a [myID](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/myid) with at least Basic identity strength
* be authorised to act on behalf of their organisation through [Relationship Authorisation Manager](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/relationship-authorisation-manager) (RAM)
* have activated their My Aged Care portal user account.
