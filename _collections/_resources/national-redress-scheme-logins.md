## Details

Direct link: [*https://www.nationalredress.gov.au/*](https://www.nationalredress.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Social Services**

The National Redress Scheme website provides support to people who experienced institutional child sexual abuse.

Users can securely login to the website using their myGov account, or choose to access and respond to emails using Department of Social Services secure mail service.
