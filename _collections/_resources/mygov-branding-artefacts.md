## Details

Responsible agency: **Services Australia**

myGov marketing communications, including media and social media, is managed in relation to the myGov digital service by Services Australia. This includes brand management, brand artefacts, the communications strategy and managing and updating myGov print and digital media products.

To learn more about myGov branding artefacts contact [MYGOV.ENGAGEMENT@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:MYGOV.ENGAGEMENT@servicesaustralia.gov.au).
