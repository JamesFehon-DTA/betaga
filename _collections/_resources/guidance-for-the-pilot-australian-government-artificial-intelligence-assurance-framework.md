## Details

*Direct link:* [*https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/ai/pilot-ai-assurance-framework/guidance*](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/ai/pilot-ai-assurance-framework/guidance)  
Responsible agency: **Digital Transformation Agency**  
Last updated: October 2024

This document provides guidance for Australian Government agencies completing assessments using the draft [Australian Government artificial intelligence (AI) assurance framework](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/pilot-australian-government-artificial-intelligence-assurance-framework). Use it as an interpretation aid and as a source of useful prompts and resources to assist you in filling out the assessment.

*The draft framework and guidance are subject to change based on feedback from pilot participants and other stakeholders. This pilot draft does not represent a final Australian Government position on AI assurance.*

## Access the guidance

The digital.gov.au website hosts the [Guidance for the Pilot Australian Government artificial intelligence assurance framework (full text)](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/ai/pilot-ai-assurance-framework/guidance).

For further information on the framework and accompanying guidance, please email [aistandards@dta.gov.au](mailto:aistandards@dta.gov.au).
