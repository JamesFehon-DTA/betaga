## Details

Direct link:[*www.ato.gov.au*](https://www.ato.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Australian Taxation Office**

The DXP is an extensible, headless platform which enables multiple business teams to develop content for internal and external client interaction websites using customisable automated workflows enabling authoring, reviews, publishing, forms delivery, analytics, reporting and digital asset management. It is extended with technology to enable pre-publishing WCAG and content rules checking, and advanced search. The platform allows greater business independence from the IT team and enables websites designed for self-service and phone call reduction.

Hosting environments:

* Sitecore XP in AWS and on-premise
* Content Hub, Coveo and Siteimprove are SaaS

Network environments

* AWS and on-premise

For more information, please contact [ESTDDSSE@ato.gov.au](mailto:ESTDDSSE@ato.gov.au).
