## Details

Direct link: [*www.dva.gov.au/myservice*](http://www.dva.gov.au/myservice)   
Responsible agency: **Department of Veterans’ Affairs**

MyService provides veterans and their families with a way to connect with and get support from the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA) online. Accessed through MyGov, people can use MyService to:

* submit claims and view their progress
* upload information to support claims
* apply for DVA-funded mental health treatment and other benefits
* view their Veteran Card online and see accepted conditions
* book transport and claim transport costs
* download official letters for tax or concession purposes
* update their personal details.
