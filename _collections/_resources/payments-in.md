## Details

*Payments in* was a digital platform that allowed individuals to pay government agencies using their preferred payment method.

The platform enabled agencies to receive paid invoices and reports without managing multiple payment types.

The [*Payments in*](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20211005022300/https:/www.dta.gov.au/our-projects/whole-government-platforms-programs-now-retired) platform has been retired and is no longer available for reuse.
