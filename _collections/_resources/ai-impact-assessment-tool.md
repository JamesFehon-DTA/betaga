## About the tool

The [AI impact assessment tool](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/impact-assessment-tool) supports agencies to assess the impacts and risks of an artificial intelligence (AI) use case against [Australia's AI ethics principles](https://www.industry.gov.au/publications/australias-artificial-intelligence-ethics-framework/australias-ai-ethics-principles) and embed effective measures to address these.

## Applicability

Non-corporate Commonwealth entities must conduct an AI use case impact assessment for AI use cases that are in scope of the [Policy for the responsible use of AI in government](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/ai/policy). Check the policy for details and exemptions.
