## Details

Direct link: [*www.education.gov.au*](https://www.education.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Education**

education.gov.au is the official website for the Department of Education. It provides news, announcements and information on programs and initiatives and resources for early childhood, primary and higher education and international education.

This website meets WCAG standards and offers several reusable design elements that can be used by other entities including:

* good user-centric design and easy access to information and resources
* intuitive and user-friendly navigation
* clear Call to Action (CTA) elements including ‘Get in touch’ and ‘Assistance using this site’.
