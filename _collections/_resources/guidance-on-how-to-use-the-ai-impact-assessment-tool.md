## About the guidance

The [Guidance on how to use the AI impact assessment tool](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/impact-assessment-tool/guidance) supports agencies completing an assessment of their AI use case using the [AI impact assessment tool](https://www.digital.gov.au/ai/impact-assessment-tool).

## Applicability

Non-corporate government Commonwealth entities must conduct an AI use case impact assessment for AI use cases that are in scope of the [Policy for the responsible use of AI in government](https://www.digital.gov.au/policy/ai/policy). Check the policy for details and exemptions.
