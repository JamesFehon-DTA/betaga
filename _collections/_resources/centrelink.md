## Details

Direct link: [*www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/centrelink-online-account*](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/centrelink-online-account)  
Responsible agency: **Services Australia**

A [Centrelink](http://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/centrelink) online account allows an individual to:

* claim payments
* manage documents
* report employment income
* manage money
* view payments and overpayments.

A [myGov](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/mygov) account is needed to set up and use a [Centrelink online account](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/centrelink-online-account).
