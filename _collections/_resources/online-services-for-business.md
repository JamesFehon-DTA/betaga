## Details

Direct link: [*www.ato.gov.au/online-services/accessing-online-services-with-digital-id-and-ram*](http://www.ato.gov.au/online-services/accessing-online-services-with-digital-id-and-ram)  
Responsible agency: **Australian Taxation Office**  
  
*Online services for business* is the default online service for approved self-managed super fund (SMSF) auditors, businesses, not-for-profits (NFPs) and other organisations to engage with the ATO. Trustees of [self-managed superannuation funds](https://www.ato.gov.au/online-services/self-managed-superannuation-funds) who have an ABN can also use this service.

To access this service, users need to sign in using a [myID](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/myid), the Australian Government's [Digital ID](https://www.digitalidsystem.gov.au/) app, linked to their business in [Relationship authorisation manager](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/relationship-authorisation-manager) (RAM).
