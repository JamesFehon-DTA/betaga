## Details

Direct link: [*www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=buyer&kb=KB0010642*](http://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=buyer&kb=KB0010642)  
Responsible agency: **Digital Transformation Agency**  
Last updated: **3 September 2021**

The Commonwealth has a whole-of-government arrangement with Microsoft to drive efficiencies in procuring Microsoft products and services. All agencies, by default, are enrolled to use the Microsoft product suite, with Microsoft Teams and SharePoint being the primary collaboration tools.

For questions about the whole-of-government arrangement for Microsoft products or services:
