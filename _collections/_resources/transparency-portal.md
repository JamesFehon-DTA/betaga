## Details

Direct link: [*www.transparency.gov.au*](http://www.transparency.gov.au)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Finance**  
  
The [*Transparency portal*](https://www.transparency.gov.au/) contains all publicly available corporate information for Commonwealth entities.

Reports available on the portal include:

* Portfolio Budget Statements
* Corporate Plans
* Annual Reports

The portal also includes data sets from annual reports and an explorer that supports comparison of data across entities and periods.

For more information, please contact [DAR@finance.gov.au](mailto:DAR@finance.gov.au).
