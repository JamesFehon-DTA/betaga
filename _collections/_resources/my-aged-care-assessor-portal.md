## Details

Direct link: *[www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/my-aged-care-assessor-portal](http://www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/my-aged-care-assessor-portal)*  
Responsible agency: **Department of Health and Aged Care**

The [*My aged care assessor portal*](https://thirdparty-3.myac.gov.au/adfs/ls/?wa=wsignin1.0&wtrealm=https%3a%2f%2fmyagedcare-assessorportal.health.gov.au%2f&wctx=rm%3d0%26id%3dpassive%26ru%3d%252f&wct=2025-04-30T05%3a31%3a12Z) is a secure portal for assessors to:

* manage assessment referrals
* conduct aged care assessments
* develop client support plans and refer clients to aged care services.

To access the *My aged care assessor portal* using myID, users need to:

* have a myID with at least Basic identity strength
* be authorised to act on behalf of their organisation through [*Relationship authorisation manager*](https://info.authorisationmanager.gov.au/manage-authorisations) (RAM)
* have activated their *My aged care portal* user account.
