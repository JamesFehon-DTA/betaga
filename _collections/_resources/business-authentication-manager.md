## Details

Responsible agency: **Australian Taxation Office**

BAM was a system that enabled government agencies to accept digital identity and business authentication from their portal. It was an integration component that allowed government agencies accept digital identity and user authentication from their portal when the Australian Taxation Office decommissioned the AUSkey service and transitioned to myGovID (now MyID). BAM was replaced by the [Services Australia Identity Exchange](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/services-australia-identity-exchange) in 2022.
