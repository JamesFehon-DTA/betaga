## Details

Direct link: [*www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=buyer&kb=KB0010649&kb\_parent=KB0010646*](http://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=buyer&kb=KB0010649&kb_parent=KB0010646)  
Responsible agency: **Digital Transformation Agency**

The DTA has established a whole-of-government arrangement with IBM Australia to simplify procurement of IBM products and services. The IBM arrangement was signed on 15 December 2022. It runs from December 2022 to December 2027. The scope includes hardware and hardware leasing, professional services, software and cloud services.
