## Details

Direct link:[*design-system.agriculture.gov.au*](https://design-system.agriculture.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry**

The Agriculture Design System (AgDS) is an open-source design system that builds on the visual language and design principles of the original Australian Government Design System (GOLD). It provides a range of components designed for information and transactional services and enables the design and development of consistent, intuitive, and accessible applications with efficiency, scale and collaboration, in line with the Digital Service Standard.

For more information, contact [capability.team@aff.gov.au](mailto:capability.team@aff.gov.au).
