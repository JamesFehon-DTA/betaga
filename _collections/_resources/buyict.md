## Details

Direct link: [*www.buyict.gov.au/sp*](http://www.buyict.gov.au/sp)  
Responsible agency: **Digital Transformation Agency**

BuyICT is a centralised online platform for government buyers to source digital products and services, find sellers and discover existing government solutions.

Underpinned by marketplaces and whole-of government agreements, BuyICT is a service portal connecting buyers from all levels of government with large, medium and small companies, including startups. Buyers list and manage opportunities on BuyICT, which approved sellers are invited to pitch and respond to.

BuyICTâ€™s panels and marketplaces regularly accept applications from new sellers and provide opportunities for existing sellers to update their offerings.

The platform also features a product and services catalogue of sellers, and provides buyers with guidance to support the digital sourcing process.
