## Details

Direct link: [*https://www.dataplace.gov.au/forms/contact-us*](https://www.dataplace.gov.au/forms/contact-us)  
Responsible agency: **Office of the National Data Commissioner**

The Dataplace website has integrated [SurveyJS](https://surveyjs.io/try/reactjs) for easy content development of new web forms, in a solution that protects the privacy of form data from third party providers.

SurveyJS use dynamic JSON-based forms in JavaScript applications to collect responses from users.
