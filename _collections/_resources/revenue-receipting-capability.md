## Details

Direct link: [*https://payment.homeaffairs.gov.au*](https://payment.homeaffairs.gov.au)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Home Affairs**  
  
The *Revenue receipting platform* is an inbound payments platform designed to receive payments and temporary payments on a large scale. It includes repayment refunds, pay later billing, POS, EFTPOS, direct debit and reversal capabilities. It uses Digital identity for a safe and secure way to prove your identity.

The platform is intended for reuse by other agencies and services. The Department of Home Affairs is developing patterns for integration and onboarding to the platform.
