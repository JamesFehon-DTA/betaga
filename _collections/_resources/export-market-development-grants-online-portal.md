## Details

Direct link: [*https://emdg.austrade.gov.au/login*](https://emdg.austrade.gov.au/login?returnUrl=%2F)  
Responsible agency: **Australian Trade and Investment Commission**   
Last updated: **December 2024**

Small to medium sized businesses can apply for an *Export market development grant* (EMDG) to help promote their Australian goods, services, IP address and know-how in overseas markets.

The [EMDG application portal](https://emdg.austrade.gov.au/login) enables applicants to:

* apply for a grant
* view the status of their application
* view and sign their grant agreement
* submit milestone reports.

Applicants are required to use their Digital Identity, myID, with standard identity strength to access the EMDG service. The applicant's myID must be linked to the business in the Relationship Authorisation Manager (RAM).
