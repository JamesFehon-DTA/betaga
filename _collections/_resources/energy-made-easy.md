## Details

Direct link: [*www.energymadeeasy.gov.au*](https://www.energymadeeasy.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Australian Energy Regulator**

Energy Made Easy is a free, independent government service from the Australian Energy Regulator (AER). The energymadeeasy.gov.au website compares energy plans from various providers. It helps individuals and small businesses find the best plan for their needs.

This website meets WCAG standards and provides reusable design elements that can be used by other entities, including user-centric design and simple navigation.
