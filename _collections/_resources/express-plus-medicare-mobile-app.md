## Details

Direct link: [*www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/express-plus-medicare-mobile-app*](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/express-plus-medicare-mobile-app)  
Responsible agency: **Services Australia**

The Express Plus Medicare app is a mobile app that lets users manage their Medicare information from their mobile devices, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

To use the app, a user must have:

* their own account for security reasons
* a myGov account linked to their Centrelink online account.

The app collects and shares information in compliance with the *Privacy Act 1988* (Cth).

Users of app can:

* check and update personal details
* access their digital Medicare card
* check their eligibility and entitlements
* manage appointments
* report changes in personal circumstances.

The app is WCAG compliant and offers several reusable design elements, including:

* an easy-to-use platform
* various accessibility functionalities
* simple navigation.
