## Details

Direct link: [*https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/contact*](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/contact)  
Responsible agency: **Services Australia**

Services Australia utilise digital assistants, powered by artificial intelligence (AI), to enhance service delivery and help users to:

* find information faster
* manage their accounts
* process some basic claims.

They are available across the following services:

* Medicare
* Centrelink
* Child Support
* myGov.

The digital assistants respond to userâ€™s enquiries through a chat function. Users are prompted to use the digital assistant by selecting the Assistant button on a web page. Once selected, the user can chat with the assistant in a pop-up chat window.

Users are reminded not to reveal any personal information, in line with the Services Australia Privacy Policy and the Privacy Act 1988.
