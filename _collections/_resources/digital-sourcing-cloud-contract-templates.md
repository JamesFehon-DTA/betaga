## Details

Direct link: [*www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=resources\_and\_policies&kb=KB0010684*](https://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=resources_and_policies&kb=KB0010684)  
Responsible agency: **Digital Transformation Agency**

The Digital Transformation Agency (DTA) provides [digital sourcing contract templates](https://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=resources_and_policies&kb=KB0010684&kb_parent=KB0010686) that make it easier for government agencies to set up contracts with suppliers.

Agencies should use the [Cloud marketplace](https://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=marketplace_landing&marketplace=20d4561edb261c106529773c349619b7&kb=KB0010616&path=buying) to source cloud services. The cloud marketplace is designed to meet the needs of government cloud buyers in most situations.

Download the [cloud sourcing contract templates](https://www.buyict.gov.au/sp?id=resources_and_policies&kb=KB0010684) from BuyICT.
