## Details

Direct link: [*www.housing.vic.gov.au/online-services*](http://www.housing.vic.gov.au/online-services)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Families, Fairness and Housing**

HousingVic Online Services is a secure online platform allowing Victorian renters and social housing applicants to view and manage their housing information through myGov.

The secure online platform allows renters to:

* make a rental payment
* request a statement
* update household income
* request maintenance
* update details
* receive digital mail
* apply for housing
* apply for a RentAssist bond loan.

The platform meets the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0](https://www.w3.org/WAI/intro/wcag.php) to AA level, according to the [World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)](https://www.w3.org/).
