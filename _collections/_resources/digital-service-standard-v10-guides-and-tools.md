## Details

> The [Digital Service Standard v2.0](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/digital-service-standard-v2) was released on 4 December 2023. For the 2024-25 Budget process, agencies going through the DCAP Assessment will be required to meet the [Digital Service Standard v1.0](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/digital-service-standard-v1).

The Digital Service Standard Guides and Tools provides advice on how agencies can work towards implementing and achieving the DSS outcomes.

View the [Digital Service Standard v1.0 guides and tools (full text)](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/digital-service-standard-v1-guides-and-tools-full-text).
