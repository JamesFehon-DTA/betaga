## Details

Direct link: [*maps.finance.gov.au/cars*](https://maps.finance.gov.au/cars)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Finance**

The [COMCAR automated resource system](https://maps.finance.gov.au/cars) (CARS) is COMCAR’s online booking and dispatch system. It provides car-with-driver transport services for parliamentarians, the Governor-General, the federal judiciary and international guests of government.

CARS enables users to manage their COMCAR bookings, and can be accessed through a mobile app or web portal.

CARS can be used to:

* make, amend or cancel COMCAR bookings
* view current, future and completed bookings in real time
* receive notifications about bookings
* manage account profiles online, including updating address and contact details
* add or update frequently used pick-up and drop off locations
* track the COMCAR’s location from dispatch to completion of the trip.
