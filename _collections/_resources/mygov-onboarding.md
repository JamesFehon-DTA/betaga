## Details

Direct link: [*https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-website/link-services-to-your-account*](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-website/link-services-to-your-account)  
Responsible agency: **Services Australia**

The myGov Engagement and Onboarding Team is the front door for all myGov enquiries, supporting new and existing Government Agencies to onboard and increase capabilities. The myGov Engagement and Onboarding team provides a tailored approach to agencies to understand and navigate the governance and compliance requirements.

To learn more about onboarding to myGov including the requirements, specifications and standards needed to integrate to myGov contact [MYGOV.ENGAGEMENT@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:MYGOV.ENGAGEMENT@servicesaustralia.gov.au).
