## Details

Direct link: [*www.health.gov.au/our-work/support-at-home*](http://www.health.gov.au/our-work/support-at-home/)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Health, Disability and Ageing**

This is a program to design, build, implement and sustain the ICT changes necessary to enable the [*Support at home* program](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/support-at-home/about#:~:text=Support%20at%20Home%20will%20be,care%20services%20for%20older%20people.). *Support at home* will bring together current in-home aged care programs, ensuring a simpler and more equitable system for older people that helps them to stay at home for longer.
