## Details

Direct link: [*afp.gov.au/news-centre/media-release/afp-has-launched-new-technology-platform-manage-investigations-next*](https://www.afp.gov.au/news-centre/media-release/afp-has-launched-new-technology-platform-manage-investigations-next)  
Responsible agency: **Australian Federal Police**

The *Investigations management solution* (IMS) is a single platform for operational members to manage investigations throughout the lifecycle, including receipt of a report or request, generation of evidence briefs, and finalisation of the investigation process. It was released in April 2023 to replace the *Police real-time online management information system* (PROMIS).

While it has elements of a [case management systems (CMS)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Case+Management+System+%28CMS%29&oq=Investigations+Management+Solution+%28IMS%29&gs_lcrp=EgRlZGdlKgYIABBFGDkyBggAEEUYOTIICAEQ6QcY_FXSAQgxNDYyajBqMagCALACAQ&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8&mstk=AUtExfCekrBkUuVkLn6a3UjSsU9A71vZ84QZNEaEFT4WOVYwk8mbJT3cE4N1Nu8AYO8g3hy-BFLkUWQWHveTqkeC-QWk1ihjHj_WYRZwbB731213u4AF8z-zN8AiSIS1Fm6ai30&csui=3&ved=2ahUKEwjohtCCre2PAxX9SmwGHeJ9HWMQgK4QegQIARAC), an IMS has advance features, as it focuses on adaptive, dynamic investigative workflows. By integrating investigative methodology, investigators are able to solve complex problems, manage delicate information, and produce court-ready evidence packages.

The key features and functionalities of the IMS are:

* evidence management
* workflow management
* case coordination
* intelligence gathering & analysis
* reporting
