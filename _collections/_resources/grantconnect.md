## Details

Direct link: [*help.grants.gov.au*](https://help.grants.gov.au/)   
Responsible agency: **Department of Finance**  
  
GrantConnect is the centralised location for Australian Government grants, where entities publish grant opportunity guidelines and awards, in line with the [*Grant management standard*](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/grant-management-standard) and [*Grant management policy*](https://architecture.digital.gov.au/grant-management-policy).

The system contains information that can be used to search for grant opportunities, forecast grant opportunities and find grants that have been awarded.
