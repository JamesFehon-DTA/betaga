## Details

Direct link: [*www.dva.gov.au*](https://www.dva.gov.au/)  
Responsible agency: **Department of Veterans’ Affairs**

The Department of Veterans' Affairs official website dva.gov.au provides key services, news and advice for veterans, their families and the broader Defence community.

The site offers resources for:

* accessing mental health support
* making claims
* getting urgent help and support
* providing support for eligible family members
* accessing information.

This website meets WCAG standards and provides several reusable design elements that can be used by other entities including:

* responsive design elements, allowing for a seamless experience across devices
* intuitive and user-friendly navigation
* clear Call to Action (CTA) elements, such as ‘Get Support’, ‘Apply Now’ etc.
* use of interactive elements, such are filters, search bars etc, to improve user experience.
