## Details

Direct link:[*https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/einvoicing/einvoicing-for-government*](https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/einvoicing/einvoicing-for-government)   
Responsible agency: **Australian Taxation Office**

This site helps Commonwealth entities adopt, and engage with suppliers on use of, eInvoicing. It includes a list of Australian Government entities able to receive eInvoices, as well as guidance on receiving and processing eInvoices.

All government agencies, including state and territory agencies and local government organisations, are welcome to use and adapt the resources on this site.

### Access the guidance

The Australian Taxation Office website hosts the [eInvoicing for government](https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/einvoicing/einvoicing-for-government) site.

For more information, please contact [eInvoicing@ato.gov.au](mailto:eInvoicing@ato.gov.au).
